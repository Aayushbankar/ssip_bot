Privacy Policy
This Policy outlines the types of information we gather about you and how we use it. We understand that your personal information is very sensitive, and protecting the privacy of this information is a top priority for us. All the information collected by this site will be kept strictly confidential. We do not rent, sell or share your personal information with any other third party.
We will not disclose your personal information with any other third party unless: • To help investigate, prevent or take action regarding unlawful and illegal activities, suspected fraud, potential threat to the safety or security of any person, violations ofhttp://www.mosdac.gov.interms of use or to defend against legal claims. • Special circumstances such as compliance with subpoenas, court orders, requests/order from legal authorities or law enforcement agencies requiring such disclosure. If we ever change the Privacy Policy, we will post that information on this page.
